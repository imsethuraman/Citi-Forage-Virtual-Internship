# Citi-Forage-Virtual-Internship
Treasury &amp; Trade Solutions – Commercial Card Analysis

📌 Project Overview

This virtual internship simulates real-world business scenarios from Citibank under the Treasury & Trade Solutions (TTS) division.

The project focuses on analyzing corporate card transactions, identifying business insights, and recommending strategies to improve profitability, customer acquisition, and risk management.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze corporate transaction data (5,000+ transactions)
Identify spending patterns, customer behavior, and revenue drivers
Evaluate pricing strategy vs industry benchmarks
Provide data-driven business recommendations

🧾 Dataset & Tasks

📊 Transaction dataset (corporate card usage)
📈 Monthly spending trends
💳 Fee structure analysis
🌍 Regional usage insights (local vs overseas)
🔍 Key Business Insights

1. Customer Distribution

Small client base (10 clients)
The top client contributes ~20% of the total spend
High dependency on a few major clients

2. Transaction Behavior
Transactions range: $0 – $50,000
Even distribution across the value range

3. Regional Trends
Local transactions = 3× overseas
Overseas transactions generate higher fees


4. Payment Mode Analysis
~50%: Cash withdrawals
~33%: Online payments
Remaining: Transfers

5. Seasonal Spending Trends
Peaks in June, July, and December

Summer period
Year-end/holidays

6. Pricing & Fee Insights
Annual fee: $1,200 (high vs market)
Generates only ~$12K revenue
Total fees: ~$700K


7. Transaction Fee Opportunity
Current % fee is low
Increasing by 1.5% → +131% revenue growth potential
📊 Strategic Recommendations
🚀 Growth Strategy
Launch aggressive marketing campaigns
Introduce referral programs

💰 Pricing Optimization
❌ Remove annual fee
